Location & Service Times

Location

The Acklen Avenue Church of Christ is located at the corner of Acklen Avenue & Wedgewood Avenue in the heart of Nashville, Tennessee.

Service Times

Worship

Sunday Morning Worship begins at 9:00 AM and usually lasts about an hour. The service includes prayer, Bible readings, songs, a sermon, and the Lord’s Supper. Afterwards, coffee and donuts are served. There is a cry room in the back of the auditorium as well as a nursery downstairs.

Sunday Evening Worship begins at 5:00 PM and usually lasts 45 minutes. The service includes prayer, Bible readings, songs, a brief sermon, and the Lord’s Supper.

Bible Study

Sunday Morning Bible Study is from 10:15-11:00 AM. We have classes for adults and children. Classes are relaxed and discussion oriented.

Wednesday Night Bible Study is from 6:30-7:30 PM. After a short time of singing and prayer in the auditorium, we break into children and adult classes.

Small Group Ministry

Jesus calls us into spiritual community. One of the ways we practice this is through small groups. Currently, we have five small groups meeting in homes. Groups meet for about an hour to share life together, pray, and discuss the Bible. Kids are always welcome.
